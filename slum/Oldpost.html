Old Post Office

Old Post Office Road is a slum area located in the city of Visakhapatnam under the jurisdiction of GVMC (Greater Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation). The slum area is home to around 10,000 residents, and like many other slum areas in India, faces several challenges that impact the well-being of its residents.

Major Problems

  • Overcrowding and poor living conditions: Many households in Old Post Office Road live in overcrowded, makeshift houses with inadequate sanitation facilities, which can lead to health issues such as the spread of infectious diseases.
  • Inadequate healthcare: Medical facilities in Old Post Office Road are often limited and of poor quality, which can limit access to healthcare for residents
  • Limited educational opportunities: Many children in Old Post Office Road do not attend school due to the lack of resources and access to quality education, which can perpetuate the cycle of poverty
