Adarsh Nagar

Adarsh Nagar is a slum settlement located in the GVMC (Greater Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation) area of Visakhapatnam, a city in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. The settlement is characterized by a lack of basic amenities such as proper sanitation, clean water supply, and adequate housing. The residents of the slum are mostly from low-income families who work in the nearby factories or as daily wage laborers
